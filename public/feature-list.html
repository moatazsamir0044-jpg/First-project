Homepage & Discovery

1.1

Full-screen Hero with Live Stats Bar

What it does

Cinematic full-screen landing section with property photo, brand headline, and a stats strip: 500+ verified properties · 10,000+ happy guests · 4.8★ average rating · 24/7 Arabic & English support.

Business value

First impressions determine bounce rate. Social proof numbers build trust with a new visitor before they scroll — reducing drop-off at the very top of the funnel.

1.2

Integrated Search Widget (in the Hero)

What it does

Destination / check-in / check-out / guests search bar embedded directly in the hero. One click sends the guest to a filtered listings page. Pre-fills sensible default dates.

Business value

Every extra click before searching costs conversions. Putting search at the very first touchpoint removes friction and gets guests into the booking funnel immediately.

1.3

Featured Listings Strip

What it does

Curated row of top-performing properties on the homepage — photo, name, location, price, and rating.

Business value

Guests who arrive without a specific destination need inspiration. Featured listings convert passive browsers into active searchers.

1.4

Destination Cards

What it does

Visual cards for the four main markets — New Cairo, North Coast / Sahel, El Gouna, Sheikh Zayed — each linking to a pre-filtered listings page.

Business value

Geographic segmentation lets guests self-identify quickly. BirdNest can promote seasonal inventory directly from the homepage without developer work.

1.5

"How It Works" Section & Dedicated Page

What it does

Three-step illustrated walkthrough (Search → Book → Stay) on the homepage, plus a full /how-it-works page with a 6-question FAQ section.

Business value

Unfamiliar guests drop off when they don't understand the process. An explicit trust-building section reduces support enquiries and booking anxiety.

1.6

"Why BirdNest" Trust Section

What it does

Four illustrated pillars — Verified Properties, Transparent Pricing, Flexible Bookings, Local Expert Support — presented visually on the homepage.

Business value

Direct answer to "why not Airbnb?" BirdNest's Egypt-specific advantages become tangible and visible rather than buried in an About page.

1.7

Testimonials Section

What it does

Curated guest reviews with names, star ratings, and photos displayed prominently on the homepage.

Business value

Social proof at the homepage level. Guests who see peer validation before searching are more likely to complete a booking.

1.8

Newsletter Signup

What it does

Email capture section at the bottom of the homepage.

Business value

Builds a direct marketing channel. Summer properties can be promoted to warm leads before the season. Email cost-per-booking is a fraction of paid acquisition.

1.9

Cookie Consent Banner (GDPR-compliant)

What it does

Consent banner on first visit — records the user's choice and does not fire analytics until consent is given.

Business value

Legal compliance for EU/UK guests. Required to run Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or retargeting campaigns lawfully.

Listings Search & Filtering

2.1

Advanced Filter Sidebar

What it does
  • Bedrooms: Studio, 1, 2, 3, 4+
  • Price range: min/max per night (EGP)
  • 12 amenity checkboxes: Pool, Gym, Beach, WiFi, AC, Kitchen, Washer, Workspace, Pet Friendly, BBQ, Garden, Parking
  • Cancellation policy: Flexible, Moderate, Non-refundable
Business value

Filtering reduces time-to-decision and stops guests from abandoning when they see irrelevant listings. Amenity filters are a top-3 UX request on any rental platform.

2.2

Sort Bar

What it does

Sort listings by price (low–high, high–low), rating, or newest arrival.

Business value

Budget travellers sort by price; quality-seekers sort by rating. The same inventory appeals to both without duplication.

2.3

Shareable / Bookmarkable Filter URLs

What it does

Every filter updates the URL (/listings?area=El+Gouna&bedrooms=2&amenities=Pool). The URL can be shared via WhatsApp or bookmarked.

Business value

Sales staff can send pre-filtered shortlists via WhatsApp. Marketing campaigns can link directly to seasonal inventory. Zero developer work needed.

2.4

Urgency Badges

What it does

Listing cards show "Only 2 left this weekend!" or "High season — filling fast" badges when inventory is limited.

Business value

Creates legitimate purchase urgency. Proven to lift booking conversion rates by 10–20% on property rental platforms.

2.5

Refund Policy Badges

What it does

Each listing card shows a colour-coded badge: "Free cancellation," "Partial refund," or "Non-refundable."

Business value

One of the top three questions guests ask before booking. Showing it on the card prevents wasted clicks and reduces abandoned checkout caused by policy surprises.

2.6

AI Listings Chatbot — "Nesty"

What it does

Floating chat widget. Guest types plain language ("I need a 2-bedroom with a pool near the beach for 4 adults in August") and Nesty responds with matching listings and direct booking links.

Business value

Converts guests overwhelmed by filters into assisted buyers. Functions as a 24/7 virtual sales agent — particularly valuable for Arabic-speaking guests unfamiliar with filter UIs.

Photo Gallery with Lightbox

4-column desktop photo grid and swipeable mobile view. Any photo opens a full-screen lightbox with keyboard/swipe navigation across all photos.

Business value

Full Amenities Grid

What it does

Visual icon grid showing all property amenities in a scannable, at-a-glance format.

Reviews Section

What it does

Guest reviews with star ratings, reviewer name, and date. Aggregated score shown prominently.

What it does

Each listing page emits structured JSON-LD data that Google reads to show star ratings, price, and availability directly in search results.

Business value

Rich snippets earn 20–30% higher click-through rates from Google. Free organic traffic — requires no ad spend.

Wishlist & Compare

4.1

Wishlist — Save for Later

What it does

Guests heart-save any listing. Saved listings appear in a dedicated /wishlist page. State persists between sessions.

Business value

60–70% of property bookings are not made on the first visit. Wishlists bring guests back. They also create a high-intent user signal useful for email retargeting.

Side-by-Side Property Comparison

What it does

Select up to 3 properties from the wishlist and open a full comparison table — pricing, bedrooms, bathrooms, amenities matrix, refund policy, and rating in a single view.

Business value

Guests comparing multiple properties are the highest-intent segment. Keeping them on-site during comparison prevents them from going to a competitor to decide.

5

Booking & Checkout

5.1

3-Click QuickCheckout

What it does

The entire booking — guest details, eligibility, payment — on one page. Three actions complete a booking: (1) fill details + select guest type, (2) tick eligibility, (3) confirm and pay.

Business value

Reducing from 4 screens to 1 is the single highest-ROI change on the site. Industry data shows checkout simplification lifts booking completion rates by 25–40%.

Before this feature: The checkout was a 4-screen wizard (Review → Details → Eligibility → Payment). Each additional screen loses approximately 20% of guests.
5.2

Nationality-Aware Eligibility Checker

What it does

Searchable dropdown of all countries in the checkout. When an Arab-national country is selected, a real-time notice explains exactly what documents may be needed.

Business value

Proactively handling the most common booking failure point — turned away at check-in due to missing documents — reduces chargebacks, complaints, and negative reviews.

5.3

Card & Bank Transfer Payment Options

What it does

Guests choose between Stripe card payment or Egyptian bank transfer. Bank transfer shows full account details and WhatsApp confirmation instructions.

Business value

Many high-value guests in Egypt prefer bank transfer over online card payment. Offering both removes a significant payment-preference barrier.

5.4

Booking Confirmation Page

What it does

After booking, guests see a branded confirmation page with booking reference, property summary, check-in instructions, and a WhatsApp support button.

Business value

Reduces "did my booking go through?" support messages. Sets clear expectations, reducing check-in friction.

8

Security (Infrastructure)

8.1

Content Security Policy (CSP)

What it does

Every HTTP response includes a policy explicitly allowlisting which external services can load scripts (Stripe, Google, Vercel, Mapbox). Any foreign script injection is blocked automatically.

Risk reduction

Eliminates XSS and Magecart-style card skimming attacks. PCI DSS and most cyber insurance policies now require or recommend CSP for e-commerce sites.

8.2

HTTPS Enforcement (HSTS)

What it does

Strict-Transport-Security header forces all connections to HTTPS for 2 years, even if a user types "http://". Submitted for browser HSTS preload lists.

Risk reduction

Prevents downgrade attacks and man-in-the-middle interception of payment data. Required for PCI compliance.

8.3

Clickjacking Protection

What it does

X-Frame-Options: DENY prevents the site from being embedded in an iframe on a third-party page.

Risk reduction

Blocks UI redress attacks where a malicious site overlays a transparent BirdNest booking form to steal credentials or payments.

8.4

Row-Level Security on Database

What it does

All 8 database tables have RLS enabled. Without an explicit access policy, no data is accessible through the public API layer.

Risk reduction

Even if a bug exposes an API endpoint, an attacker cannot read other users' bookings, payment records, or personal data. Fundamental data protection.

8.5

Secure Password Storage (scrypt)

What it does

Passwords hashed using scrypt (NIST-recommended). Comparison uses constant-time verification to prevent timing attacks.

Risk reduction

If the database is ever compromised, stolen hashes cannot be reversed. Protects users who reuse passwords across sites.

8.6

Rate Limiting on Authentication

What it does

Registration and sign-in endpoints limit attempts per IP address within a time window. Registration never reveals whether an email already exists.

Risk reduction

Blocks credential-stuffing and brute-force attacks. Protects guest accounts and BirdNest's platform reputation.

SEO & Performance

9.1

Open Graph & Social Cards

What it does

Every page has correctly configured Open Graph and Twitter Card metadata. Sharing a BirdNest link on WhatsApp, Facebook, or LinkedIn shows a photo, title, and description automatically.

Business value

What it does

Homepage and listing pages emit JSON-LD markup that Google reads to generate rich search results showing prices, ratings, and property details.

Business value

Rich snippets increase organic click-through rates by 20–30% at zero cost. This compounds — better CTR improves ranking, which improves CTR further.

Optimised Images & Fonts

All images use Next.js Image (WebP conversion, lazy loading, correct sizing). Fonts are subset and served from the CDN edge.

Business value

Core Web Vitals are a direct Google ranking factor. Fast-loading pages rank higher and convert better — particularly on mobile connections in Egypt.

Backend & Admin

11.1

Full Admin API

What it does

Protected admin endpoints for dashboard analytics, listing management (create/update/delete), and booking oversight.

Business value

Gives the operations team tools to manage inventory without touching the database directly. Reduces technical dependency for day-to-day operations.

11.2

Complete REST API Layer

What it does

All site functionality is backed by a full API: listings search, individual listing, reviews, availability calendar, bookings, payments, contact form, and chat.

Business value

API-first architecture means a mobile app, WhatsApp chatbot, or third-party integration can be added later without rebuilding the backend.

11.3

Stripe Webhook Handler

What it does

A secure webhook endpoint that receives payment confirmation events directly from Stripe and automatically updates booking status server-side.

Business value

Bookings confirmed by Stripe's infrastructure — not by the frontend. Prevents fraud where a user manipulates the browser to skip the payment step.
